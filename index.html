Our Mission

The aims and objectives of RSH is to provide food, shelter, and education for the needy and homeless children of Ghana, and even beyond the scope Africa.

We believe that no matter the condition, children should be loved, nurtured and provided education with modern faculties.

Our Vision

Through our trainings, individuals and our stakeholders support programs, the vision of RSH is to nurture and grow the needy and homeless to become self-reliant, and fitting into the economy and the demands of nature.

We train our kids to become self-reliant through the hard work of our selfless staff and mentors

The Children's Home

Royal Seed Home and School is to build and better the lives of its children by providing nutrition, medical care, education and the opportunity to acquire transferable vocational skills. We strive to help any child in need and prepare each to be self reliant, positive, and respectful to impact the community and Ghana.

Medical Care

The health and wellbeing of the children is a top priority for us at Royal Seed. We provide medical care for the children from daily health checks at our clinic, to; transporting more serious cases to the hospital. every child at our home receives the care they need.

"To every child; I dream of a world where you can laugh, dance, sing, learn, live in peace and be happy."

Royal Seed Home (RSH) is a Residential children's home based in Ofaakor, Ghana, West Africa.
RSH provides food, shelter and education for the needy and homeless. Currently, RSH has 53 orphans and 80 community care children.
In total, 133 children and old aged, are under Royal Seed care. Royal Seed Home was founded by Naomi Esi Amoah on March 22, 2002, and registered as a non governmental organization.
